Return JSON with:
- decision: "move_to" | "stay_idle" | "return_to_base" | "continue_current"
- target_sector_id: (optional) sector ID if moving
- reasoning: A clear, concise sentence explaining WHY this decision was made (e.g., "Sector 42 is critical and unassigned", "Closest active fire").
- priority: "LOW" | "MEDIUM" | "HIGH" | "URGENT"
